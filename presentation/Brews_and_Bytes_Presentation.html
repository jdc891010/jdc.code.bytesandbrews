The Vision

Brews & Bytes is a platform built on the belief that the best workspace insights come from the community itself. We use crowdsourced data and targeted feedback to transform local cafes into optimized professional hubs.

Crowdsourced Wisdom

Community reports and questionnaires that identify why users work (or don't work) at specific venues.

More than Coffee

Finding the "best place for the task"—covering cafes, coworking hubs, and community workspaces.

Business Solutions

Providing businesses with data-driven insights to optimize their revenue and infrastructure.

Marketing & Outreach

Scaling through community engagement and professional incentives.

Social Channels

Targeted campaigns on IG, FB, and TikTok focusing on the "Remote Work Lifestyle."

Tech Boards

Engaging directly with devs and nomads on Reddit, Hacker News, and niche boards.

Incentivized Data
